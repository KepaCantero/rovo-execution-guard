## Analysis Instructions

Assess whether the ticket and its documentation are aligned. Show what is consistent as well as what is mismatched.

When relationship context is provided, use these structural signals:

1. **Direct documentation**: Does a `documented-by` edge exist from this ticket to a Confluence page? This is stronger evidence than a keyword match. Reference the specific page title and URL.

2. **Staleness**: Compare the documentation's last update with the ticket's last update. Flag drift greater than 30 days as a staleness concern. Cite specific dates.

3. **Coverage gaps**: Does the ticket's epic have a spec page? Do all sibling stories reference it? Reference specific epic and sibling keys when identifying gaps.

4. **Orphaned specs**: Are there Confluence pages that document related topics but are not linked to any ticket in the epic? These may indicate missing tickets or stale documentation.

When relationship context is absent or incomplete, proceed with direct ticket-to-documentation comparison. Do not assume that missing edges mean no documentation exists — note any uncertainty in your assessment.

Your role is advisory — suggest improvements to reduce rework from misaligned specs, rather than blocking workflow.
